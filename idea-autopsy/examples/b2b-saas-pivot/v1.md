# Stellate — From spreadsheet to internal app in 60 seconds

## Vision

Every operations team in the world maintains the same artifact: a Google Sheet that drives a process. Order tracking. Vendor management. Onboarding. Hiring pipelines. The sheet works until it doesn't — and at the moment it stops working, the team is faced with the choice of a slow IT request (months) or a no-code build (Retool, Glide, Bubble — all of which require leaving the spreadsheet).

Stellate is a Chrome extension that turns any Google Sheet into a usable internal app in 60 seconds. No data migration. No platform-leaving. We read the sheet structure, generate forms, lists, and admin views, and host them as a web app the team can use.

## Why this matters

There are ~50M Google Workspace business users. Conservatively, 20% of them maintain at least one process-driving sheet. That's 10M latent app builders. At $30/user/month for Stellate Pro, that's a $3.6B TAM.

## How it works

1. Install Chrome extension.
2. Open any sheet you want to "appify."
3. Click "Generate app." We read the schema, infer column types, propose a form + list view.
4. User edits the generated views in our drag-and-drop builder.
5. Publish to a Stellate URL. The sheet remains source-of-truth; we sync bidirectionally.
