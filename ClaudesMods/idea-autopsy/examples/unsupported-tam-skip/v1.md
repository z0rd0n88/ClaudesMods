# YieldVault — DeFi yield for your retirement account

## The opportunity

Americans hold $13 trillion in IRA and 401(k) retirement accounts. The average IRA earns ~7% annually in a balanced index portfolio. Meanwhile, DeFi protocols (Aave, Compound, Curve) consistently offer 5–15% yield on stablecoins with low correlated risk.

What if retirement savers could route a portion of their account into DeFi yield without leaving their existing retirement custodian?

## The product

YieldVault is a Chrome extension + browser-side bridge that lets users:

1. Connect their existing retirement-account portal (Fidelity, Schwab, Vanguard).
2. Designate a portion (1–10%) of their balance as "DeFi-eligible."
3. Our smart contract custodies the DeFi-eligible portion and routes it to the highest-yielding stablecoin pool (Aave / Compound / Curve).
4. Yield accrues in stablecoin, swept weekly back to the retirement account.

## Market

- $13T in US retirement accounts.

- Even 0.1% adoption × 2% average allocation × 8% yield differential = massive value capture.
